Getting to the Venue

Qingdao is well connected by flights and high-speed rail. For the most accurate and real-time trip planning, please use Amap route links below.

  • From Qingdao Jiaodong International Airport (TAO) to Huanghai Hotel: Amap Route
  • From Qingdao Railway Station (青岛站) to Huanghai Hotel: Amap Route
  • From Qingdao North Railway Station (青岛北站) to Huanghai Hotel: Amap Route
Taxi / Ride-hailing (Reference)
  • TAO airport to venue area: fastest route is approximately 57 min for 58 km; estimated ride-hailing fare is about RMB 85–115, and expressway tolls may apply (reference: around RMB 17).
  • Qingdao Railway Station (青岛站) to Huanghai Hotel: fastest route is approximately 8 min for 3.7 km; estimated ride-hailing fare is about RMB 10–12.
  • Qingdao North Railway Station (青岛北站) to Huanghai Hotel: fastest route is approximately 48 min for 15.4 km; estimated ride-hailing fare is about RMB 33–45 (taxi estimate in reference screenshot: around RMB 65).
  • Pickup tip at Qingdao Railway Station: use "青岛站-北侧-泰安路-金海大酒店门口" as the pickup point in Amap/DiDi for easier driver matching.
Estimated travel times and fares are for reference only (as of March 2026) and may vary by traffic, weather, and time period. Please check Amap/DiDi for real-time details before departure.
Visa Information
